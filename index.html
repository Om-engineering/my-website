About Us

Established over two decades ago in Pali, Rajasthan, New Om Engineering is a trusted manufacturer of industrial machinery. We specialize in providing innovative solutions for the food, marble, and textile industries.

Our expertise includes custom-designed machines such as industrial boilers, commercial lifts, and specialized manufacturing units. With a commitment to quality and precision, we ensure every project meets the highest industry standards.
